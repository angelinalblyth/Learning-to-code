Chester Bennington

This is a tribute page to the late Chester Bennington, lead singer for the rock band Linkin Park.

Chester has been a part of a few bands -

  • Grey Daze
  • Linkin Park
  • Dead by Sunrise
  • Stone Temple Pilots

below will be a short discription's of his work with these bands.

Grey Daze

Chester first began singing with a band named Sean Dowdell and his Friends? but later moved to Grey Daze with Sean Dowdell, a post-grunge band from Phoenix Arizona. The band recorded three albums but Chester left in 1998 but struggled to find another band.

Linkin Park

Chester was frustrated at not being able to find a band after leaving Grey Daze and even thought about quitting singing. He was offered an audition for Linkin Park by the vice president of Zomba Music in Los Angeles. He had a successful audition for the band, named at the time Xero, and managed to record the song for his audition in a day. After failing to find a record deal and facing numerous rejections they finally landed a deal with Warner Bros. Records In 2000 Linkin Park released their debut album Hybrid Theory.

Chester was a member of Linkin Park for 17 years, they relased 7 albums as well as many side projects. They toured all over the world.

Dead by sunrise

Chester co-founded Dead by Sunrise in 2005 with a few of his friends that where part of other bands at the time. They made their debut live in May 2008, performing four songs and they later released their debut album Out of Ashes on October 2009

Stone Temple Pilots

In February 2013 STP parted ways with their lead singer and the band recruited Chester as a replacement later in May 2013, he took to the stage for the first time in May 2013 with the band. Chester reflected on joining Stone Temple Pilots, stating, "Every band has its own kind of vibe. Stone Temple Pilots has this sexier, more classic rock feel to it. Linkin Park is a very modern, very tech-heavy type of band. I grew up listening to these guys. When this opportunity came up, it was just like a no-brainer." Bennington stated in interviews that singing lead vocals in Stone Temple Pilots was his lifelong dream. He left the band on good terms due to his commitments with Linkin Park in 2015

Chesters Death

Chester died on July 20th 2017; his death ruled a suicide by hanging. Mike another member of Linkin Park confirmed his death on his twitter writing "Shocked and heartbroken, but it's true. An official statement will come out as soon as we have one" The band will be doing a tribute concert for Chester and will take place October 27 at the Hollywood Bowl in Los Angeles.
